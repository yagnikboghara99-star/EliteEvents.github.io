Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our event planning services

How far in advance should I book your services?

+

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for weddings and large corporate events, and 3-6 months for smaller events. However, we can accommodate shorter timelines based on availability.

What's included in your event planning packages?

+

Our packages vary by service level but typically include venue coordination, vendor management, timeline creation, day-of coordination, and custom design elements. See our Services page for detailed package information.

Do you work with specific vendors or can I choose my own?

+

We work with both approaches. We have trusted vendor partnerships that can provide excellent service and competitive pricing, but we're also happy to coordinate with vendors of your choosing.

What geographic areas do you serve?

+

We're based in New York but serve the entire tri-state area and are available for destination events worldwide. Travel costs may apply for events outside our primary service area.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Cancellation policies vary by package and timeline. Generally, cancellations more than 90 days in advance receive a partial refund, while cancellations closer to the event date are subject to vendor cancellation fees.

Do you offer payment plans?

+

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans to make our services accessible. Typically, we require a deposit to secure your date, with the balance divided into manageable installments leading up to your event.
